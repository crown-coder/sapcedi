About Us

Brief History!

The save the less Privileged and Child Education initiative (SAPCEDI) is a civil society based organization based in Billiri, about 60kms away from Gombe town, the capital of Gombe state. it was established in 2005 by a team of three (3) founding members as an organization focusing on less privilleged childred and orphans. this was with a clear focus of providing the less privileged children with Education, Reproductive Health Services particularly STI/HIV/AIDS

Vision

A world where every child has access to basic needs, education, healthcare system and safe environment. Provide access to basic needs such as food, shelter, health and education to less privileged Children and their caregivers while promoting environmental sustainability.

Mission

As a result of SAPCEDI education Programme an increase proportion of less privileged, orphans and vulnerable children (OVC), people would have adopted a healthy, educated, and empoered partners to demand and achieve their basic needs and right.

Our Services

We provide!

Healthy Food

We provide nutritious meals to ensure every child has access to healthy food, helping them grow strong and thrive in their communities.

Education

Our programs offer quality education and learning resources to empower children with the knowledge and skills they need for a brighter future.

Pure Water

We deliver clean and safe drinking water to children and their families, protecting them from waterborne diseases and promoting good health.

Medical

We provide medical care and health services to ensure that every child has access to essential healthcare and can enjoy a healthy life.

Clothes

We distribute clothing to underprivileged children, giving them warmth, comfort, and dignity through every season of the year.

About Us

Brief History!

The save the less Privileged and Child Education initiative (SAPCEDI) is a civil society based organization based in Billiri, about 60kms away from Gombe town, the capital of Gombe state. it was established in 2005 by a team of three (3) founding members as an organization focusing on less privilleged childred and orphans. this was with a clear focus of providing the less privileged children with Education, Reproductive Health Services particularly STI/HIV/AIDS

Vision

A world where every child has access to basic needs, education, healthcare system and safe environment. Provide access to basic needs such as food, shelter, health and education to less privileged Children and their caregivers while promoting environmental sustainability.

Mission

As a result of SAPCEDI education Programme an increase proportion of less privileged, orphans and vulnerable children (OVC), people would have adopted a healthy, educated, and empoered partners to demand and achieve their basic needs and right.

Our Goal

Goals

  • Reduce Stigma on the Poor and Vulnerable
  • Education for the less Privileged, orphans and Vulnerable Children.
  • Increase Knowledge on HIV/AIDS
  • Health Consciousness for the Poor

Objectives

  • To provide education opportunities to less privileged children.
  • To provide basic needs such as food, shelter, and clothing to less privileged children.
  • To provide environmental sustainability through advocacy and community engagement.
  • To provide qualitative healthcare to less privilege children.
  • To create awareness about the importance of environmental sustainability and its impact on lives of children.

Our Services

We provide!

Healthy Food

We provide nutritious meals to ensure every child has access to healthy food, helping them grow strong and thrive in their communities.

Education

Our programs offer quality education and learning resources to empower children with the knowledge and skills they need for a brighter future.

Pure Water

We deliver clean and safe drinking water to children and their families, protecting them from waterborne diseases and promoting good health.

Medical

We provide medical care and health services to ensure that every child has access to essential healthcare and can enjoy a healthy life.

Clothes

We distribute clothing to underprivileged children, giving them warmth, comfort, and dignity through every season of the year.

Financial Support

We offer financial assistance to families in need, helping them cover essential expenses and providing stability for their children’s future.

About the Program Manager

Mustapha Abubakar is a first-class graduate of Botany from Gombe State University (GSU) and is currently pursuing his Master’s degree in Plant Ecology. He founded the Botanical Students Association of Nigeria (BOSA), Gombe State University Chapter, where he served as the pioneer president from 2020 to 2023. As an environmental specialist, Mustapha has coordinated numerous tree-planting campaigns across various regions, demonstrating his commitment to ecological preservation and sustainability. He has been recognized with several awards of merit from different organizations in appreciation of his dedication and outstanding efforts in environmental advocacy. In his role as Program Manager, Mustapha brings his extensive experience in environmental management, leadership, and community engagement to ensure the successful implementation of programs that provide vital support to underprivileged children. His passion for environmental conservation and his strong organizational skills make him an invaluable asset to our team.
